Hobbies

Traveling is my hobby and I have had the opportunity to explore many beautiful and diverse places within my country. From the remote and rugged terrain of Amiyakhum and Nafakhum in the Bandarban region, to the cascading waterfalls and lush greenery of the deep forest, each destination has provided me with a unique and unforgettable experience.

One of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my travels was the 5-6 day trek through the mountains to reach Amiyakhum and Nafakhum. These areas are located deep within Bandarban and are not easily accessible by road. The journey was physically demanding, but the breathtaking views and the feeling of accomplishment upon reaching our destination made it all worth it.

In addition to these remote locations, I have also visited many deep waterfalls that are hidden within the forest. The trek to these natural wonders was equally challenging, but the reward of standing at the base of a powerful waterfall and feeling the mist on my face was truly indescribable. I have also visited Sunamgonj and many border areas, each with its own unique culture and landscape.

One of the things I love most about traveling is the opportunity to experience different cultures and ways of life. Each place I visit has its own distinct customs, traditions, and foods. The local people have always been welcoming and friendly, and I have had the chance to learn so much from them.

Overall, traveling has been one of the most fulfilling hobbies I have ever undertaken. The experiences I have had and the places I have visited have broadened my perspective and deepened my appreciation for the natural beauty and diversity of my country. I look forward to many more travels in the future, and the memories and experiences I will gain along the way.
